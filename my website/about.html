iPhone 13

The iPhone 13 is Apple's latest smartphone, featuring advanced technology and an improved design.

Key Features:

  • 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display
  • A15 Bionic chip for faster performance
  • Dual 12 MP camera system with Night mode
  • Up to 19 hours of video playback
  • 5G support for faster internet speeds

The iPhone 13 comes in various colors, including pink, blue, midnight, starlight, and Product Red. It offers improved battery life, better durability with Ceramic Shield, and runs on iOS 15.
